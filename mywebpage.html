Thomas J. Watson

Thomas J. Watson , Sr. is the American industrialist, who built the International Business Machines Corporation(IBM) into the largest manufacturer of electric typewriters and data-processing equipmenty in the world

  • In 1939 he received an honorory degree in Doctor of Commercial Science from Oglethrope University
  • In 1940s, Watson was on the national executive board of the Boy Scouts ofAmerica
  • Watson served as a trustee of Columbia University from June6, 1933, until his death.
  • He was posthmously inducted into the U.S. Business Hall of Fame in 1990.
